Location Information



Description

The Rocky Face Cottage is located in the North Carolina mountains at the city of Taylorsville. This cottage is found looking over a mountain side facing the adjacent forested area. Enclosed between cattle and horse pastures this quiet stretch of land is where peace and calamity are created. Not too far from the cottage are excursions, downtown Taylorsville, community churches, pastures, and plenty of hiking trails. With Charlotte being an hour away you can still have a exciting vacation and a serene home away from home.
